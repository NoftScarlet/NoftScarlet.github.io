The COVID-19 pandemic has changed the world as you knew it.

Brain injury survivors are in urgent need your support during these uncertain times, and action is needed now.
The Vancouver Brain Injury Association relies on generous donations from people like you to continue offering support, advocacy, and recovery help to brain injury survivors. You can help some of the most vulnerable people with the click of a button.
